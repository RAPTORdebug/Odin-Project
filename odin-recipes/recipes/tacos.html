Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish that combines seasoned meat, fresh vegetables, and flavorful toppings all wrapped in a soft or crunchy tortilla. They're quick to prepare and easy to customize, making them a go-to for weeknight dinners or casual gatherings.

This simple taco recipe uses ground beef or chicken and classic toppings like lettuce, cheese, and salsa — perfect for beginners or anyone craving a fast, tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or chicken
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade)
  • ½ cup water
  • 8 small taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ½ cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup salsa or hot sauce (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro or lime wedges for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté for 2–3 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds, then add the ground beef or chicken. Cook until browned and fully cooked through.
  3. Drain excess fat if needed. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  4. Warm taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions or in a hot dry pan.
  5. Spoon the meat mixture into the taco shells.
  6. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomato, sour cream, salsa, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and a squeeze of lime, if using. Serve immediately and enjoy!
