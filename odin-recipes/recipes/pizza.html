Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish known for its delicious combination of a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and endless topping possibilities. From classic Margherita to spicy pepperoni or veggie-loaded options, pizza is a versatile meal that can be customized to suit anyone's taste.

This recipe will walk you through making homemade pizza from scratch — including the dough, sauce, and baking tips. Whether you're cooking for a group or treating yourself, making pizza at home is both fun and rewarding!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (about 110°F / 45°C)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 to 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Fresh basil or oregano (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm water. Sprinkle the yeast on top and let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8–10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out into your desired shape on a floured surface.
  7. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza peel. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the crust.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly on top, then add your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy!
